How to Play

Basic Controls

Move your fish with the mouse cursor.

Eat smaller fish to grow bigger.

Avoid larger fish or you'll be eaten!

Leveling Up

Earn points to advance to the next level.

Each level increases difficulty.

The progress bar at the top shows your progress to the next level.
